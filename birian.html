How to Cook Biryani

Details on how to cook Biriani...

Ingredients:
- 1 cup basmati rice
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes)
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 1 tablespoon biryani spice mix
- 1 onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 2 cups water
- Fresh cilantro and mint leaves for garnish
- Salt to taste
Instructions:
1. Rinse the rice and soak it for 30 minutes, then drain.
2. Heat oil in a large pot. Saut√© the onions until golden brown.
3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
4. Stir in the biryani spice mix and cook for another minute.
5. Add the yogurt and cook until the oil separates.
6. Add the rice and water, and season with salt.
7. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and cook on low for 20 minutes or until rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and mint before serving.
